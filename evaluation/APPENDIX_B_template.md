### CRediT category cheat-sheet

- **Conceptualization** — Ideas, framing of the project, formulation of goals.
- **Methodology** — Design or development of the analytical approach.
- **Software** — Programming, code design, implementation.
- **Validation** — Verification of reproducibility and outputs (e.g. notebook 08).
- **Formal Analysis** — Application of statistical / metric techniques to analyse data.
- **Investigation** — Conducting experiments and data collection.
- **Resources** — Provision of compute, materials, datasets.
- **Data Curation** — Cleaning and maintenance of data.
- **Writing — Original Draft** — Preparation of the technical report draft.
- **Writing — Review & Editing** — Critical review, commentary, revision.
- **Visualization** — Preparation of figures, plots, dashboards.
- **Supervision** — Oversight of the project (rarely applies in student teams).
- **Project Administration** — Coordination, scheduling, repository hygiene.

> In this work, we have employed the following Generative AI tools:
